# Backlog

This folder holds actionable work that has been intentionally deferred.

## What Goes Here

- Plans that were started but intentionally paused
- Follow-up work identified during implementation
- Future improvements that are well-defined but not yet prioritized
- Work deferred from active plans with a clear reason

## What Does Not Go Here

- Completed work (use `completed/`)
- Vague ideas without actionable scope (track elsewhere or discard)
- Research outputs (use `references/`)
- Reports (use `reports/`)

## Lifecycle

1. Item is created or moved here from `active/` when deferred
2. Include a note explaining why it was deferred
3. When ready to resume, move back to `active/`
4. Periodically review and prune stale items
